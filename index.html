I'm Priyanka Bammanahalli
I'm a
  • UX designer
  • Frontend Developer

As a beginner in the world of computer science, one area that excites me the most is web development. The idea of creating websites and web applications from scratch and seeing them come to life on the internet is truly fascinating to me.

I'm just getting started with learning the basics, like HTML for structuring web pages, CSS for styling them, and JavaScript for adding interactivity. These foundational languages form the backbone of web development, and I'm eager to deepen my understanding of each one.

I'm also exploring beginner-friendly frameworks and libraries like Bootstrap, which make it easier to create responsive and dynamic web experiences without diving too deep into complex coding.

What I find particularly intriguing about web development is its creative aspect. Designing user interfaces, choosing color schemes, and arranging elements on a page to create a seamless user experience is something I'm looking forward to exploring further.

Even though I'm just starting out, I'm excited about the potential of web development to empower me to bring my ideas to life and share them with the world. Whether it's building a personal blog, a portfolio website, or a simple web-based game, I'm eager to dive in and start creating.
